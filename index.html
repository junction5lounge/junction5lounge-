Our Story

The Heartbeat of
Adenta Nights

Nestled in the vibrant Adenta neighbourhood, Junction 5 Lounge was born from a simple idea: Accra deserves a venue where great food, creative cocktails, and live music converge in one unforgettable space.

Whether you're stopping by for lunch, gathering with friends for dinner, or celebrating into the early hours, every visit feels like an occasion. Our warm, moody interiors set the stage, our live bands and DJs keep the energy high, and our kitchen delivers plate after plate of flavour.
